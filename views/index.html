Our Mission

Our vision and mission for the future is to end homelessness and hunger.

Our goals and values

We truly believe in our vision of ending homelessness and hunger.
All people should have the right to a permanent shelter where one calls home.
We believe in taking action. We know failing is a possibility, we will use them as a stepping stone towards out goal.
We will act with the utmost respect and integrity for all parties involved as we work towards our goal as we believe respect and integrity is a basic human right.
We believe in providing hope and opportunity for those who are struggling.

We seek to take a leadership role in opening doors to greater equity, success and opportunity for marginalized Canadians struggling in homelessness and housing instability. We are vocal advocates for the voice of lived experience and the empowerment of people experiencing homelessness.

Our impact so far

We've helped millions of homeless people off the streets by providing them career opportunities with our partners and donors. Our impact reach across the nation with over 300 branches spanning across Canada. Each branch has passionate and caring members who are sharing and working towards the same goal as we are as an organization. We've helped thousands of homeless teenagers and helped them obtain and education and worked with hundreds of thousands of families at risk of poverty and homelessness to alleviate their situation. On each and every step of our journey, we strive to equally provide any opportunities that we can to those who need it the most. We've helped out thousands of communities suffering from loss of employment due to COVID-19.
