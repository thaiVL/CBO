Get to know us

We don't judge, we just help those who need it. We are a non-profit organization that began in Canada with a group of friends who were passionate about helping those who are less fortunate. At the beginning, we were only a group of 5 friends that were helping out our local community. Over time, we expanded and started an organization so that we can reach out and help others who are in farther communities than our local area. We only want to see the smile on those who we've help. Now, we are an organization that has several branches across the world, and able to offer help in many areas other than only shelter and food.

What we do

We strive to provide any necessary services to those who need it. We hope to end homelessness and allow everyone be become self-sufficient

Food services
Housing and Shelter
Education

Employment opportunities

Our Vision and Mission

Our vision and mission is to end homelessness and hunger

To achieve our vision and mission, we have a set of values and beliefs that we follow

We truly believe in our vision of ending homelessness and hunger.
All people should have the right to a permanent shelter where one calls home.
We believe in taking action. We know failing is a possibility, we will use them as a stepping stone towards out goal.
We will act with the utmost respect and integrity for all parties involved as we work towards our goal as we believe respect and integrity is a basic human right.
We believe in providing hope and opportunity for those who are struggling.

Our Team

Mr. Schrute first worked as a salesman under a paper distribution company. Then one day Mr. Schrute saw the less fortunate on the streets and thought of how fortunate his life is. At that moment, Mr. Schrute gathered a group of his friends to help the homeless around his local community.
