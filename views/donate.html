What we do with your donations!

We believe in being completely transparent in what we do with your kind donations!

If you would like a more comprehensive break down of how your donations are being used, please download this PDF for the previous year's donation funds usage.

There are several categories that we use a large majority of the funds on, some of which are

Shelter

We believe that everyone should have a place to rest and feel safe! So each year, we spend a large portion of donation funds on supporting our shelters around the nation. These funds are used in helping to pay for building maintenance, rent, repairs, improvements and much more! We make sure to create a safe and comfortable environment for all people who come to our shelter.

Basic living necessities

Basic living necessities include food, water, and clothing. These three items are of utmost importance to all those who come seek help. We want to make sure that they are well-fed, have water and adequate clothing to protect them from the cold Canadian winters. We also spend a large portion of the donations on food to send them out for those who need it as well as buying food for our shelters so that all can be well-fed.

Education

We truly believe that education provide opportunities. As such, we strive to provide as much education as possible to those who need it. We support all those who need an education, no matter what age they are.

Health Care

There are many who lack health care insurance, so one of our goal is to provide adequate health care funds for those who need it so that they do not become gravely ill. We place great importance on patients who are suffering from a grave disease that require urgent treatment but do not have the money or insurance to help pay off the medical bills.
