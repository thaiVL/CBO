Have Questions? Here are some FAQs

  • What exactly is homelessness??
    • Homelessness describes the situation of an individual or family without stable, permanent, appropriate housing, or the immediate prospect, means and ability of acquiring it
  • What causes a person to become homeless??
    • There are many many many factors that causes a person to become homeless. Some prominent ones are debt, job loss leading to permanent unemployment, mental or physical health problems and severe trauma. If you know anyone who is experience these things, please reach out and help them!
  • I really want to help, how can I help??
    • Well, I'm glad you asked! You can help us by taking a looking at our donate page!
  • I'm with a news organization, may I interview you?
    • Of course! We'd love to help spread awareness about the problem that we are tackling! Please just contact us by submitting a form below with the appropriate subject heading!
  • How do you collect data on homelessness?
    • Well, there are several ways we do this: we often get help from a researcher who specializes in sociology and they conduct the data collection for us. We also register all persons who we help so that we know who they are in the future so everything can go smoothly for their next visit!
  • I tried to access a link on this website, but it's broken!
    • We apologize for any broken links, please report them to helphomelessness@helpus.ca
  • Can I share information that I have obtained from this website?
    • Of course! We just ask that you do not commercialize any information that you may find on this website as it will infringe on our copyrights!
