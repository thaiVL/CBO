Don't be afraid to ask for help!

If you or someone you know who is at risk of being homeless or hunger, please contact us and ask for help! We are always happy to help anyone who needs it. We are a judgement-free organization and always work our hardest to strive to provide the best help. It's in our goals and values to provide the best help and opportunities we can.

Our Services

Shelter

We are able to provide those in need with temporary shelters at some of our locations. Just come on in and talk to one of our staff members or volunteers

  • 123 Confederation Street
  • 409 Street East
  • 411 Downtown Area
  • 109 Business District
Food

Everyone deserves to be able to access basic needs such as food and water. As such, we are able to provide those in need with food and water at some of our locations as listed in the shelter section. Please come by and talk with our staff members or volunteers. If you are unable to come to our location, please send us an inquiry or call us.

Clothing

Every year, we collect donated clothing from generous people of the community and gift it to those who are unable to afford adequate clothing. Come by to one of our locations and talk with a staff member who can take your measurements and arrange the correct clothing. Please understand that there is a limit on our resources, so we are able to only provide at most 2 sets of clothing per person.

Education

We believe education should be a basic right for all. As such, we will try to provide those who wants to pursue an education with adequate or cover most of their tuition. Please send an inquiry regarding this program so that we may contact you and see if you are eligible to participate in this program.

Employment opportunities

We do our best to provide internal employment opportunities to those who are struggling to obtain employment elsewhere. We do this to provide experience and qualifications as well as helping them build connections. If we are not able to offer internal employment, we will try to find external employment opportunities for candidates to apply to.
